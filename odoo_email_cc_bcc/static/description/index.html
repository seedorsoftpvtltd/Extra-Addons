Email CC is Carbon Copy of mail which means that the email address added in CC section also receives the copy of the mail. All recipients are aware of the others receiving a 'CC of the mail'. It helps you to make everybody aware of the mail trail communication without any ambiguity.

On the other hand, talking about the BCC; it is known as the Blind Carbon Copy. Similar to the CC, here also the email address added in the CC section receives the copy of the mail. You can use BCC when you need to send the same information to a group of people and it's not apt to share the email addresses of everyone in that group with one another.

Now, you can manage Email CC and BCC in Seedor without any hassle. Seedor Email Cc and Bcc Module facilitates you to include any email address and not just partners in Cc and Bcc field while composing emails in Seedor. It adds a CC and Bcc field to enter the desired single or multiple email addresses and send the mail.

Now manage Email CC and BCC in Seedor without any hassle

Seedor has solutions for most of the business problems. Now, you can enhance the mailing part by adding the functionality of CC and BCC in Seedor.

The user can set default email addresses for Cc and Bcc to appear automatically in the mails. Moreover, it also provides the user with a 'Reply-To' field to enter the email address of the correspondent if the recipient needs to reply to the sender.

It makes the conversation trackable for everyone and avoids future ambiguity.

Highlighted Features

  • It allows the user to keep track of all the correspondences in the Odoo.
  • The user can keep any desired email address in Cc and Bcc fields along with the partners in Odoo.
  • The user can also add a 'Reply-To' field to enter a custom email address of the correspondent for the recipient's use.
  • A default email address can be set to Cc and Bcc fields which would then appear automatically in the respective field while composing mails in Seedor.
  • Similarly, a default email address can be added to the Reply-To field to be used automatically in the mails
