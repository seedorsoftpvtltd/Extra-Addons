Sale Order Free/Sample Product

Do you want to send a free product sample to your clients to try new products? This module used to define free products in the sale order, quotation & invoice. Free sample products help you to improve product selling. We provide configuration for free product tax on-off. You can provide a free product using a tickbox in a single click. Cheers!

How it works? click here to watch video

Features

You can send a free product sample to your clients.

You can configure free product tax, If yes than tax calculates & If no then it will not calculate tax on free product.

You can define free products in the sale order, quotation & invoice.

Free sample products help you to improve the marketing graph.

You can define a free product in a single click.

No special configuration required, install it, use it.

This module saves your important time.

It reduces human efforts.

Version

v13.0.1 (16th May 2020)
  • Initial Release

Go to sales configuration => settings, select "Yes" in free sample product tax for free product option.

In the quotation, we have not selected a free product tickbox so it shows the total price.

Now, we tick a free product tickbox so it shows the total price is 0(Zero).

In the invoice, we have not selected a free product tickbox so it shows the total price.

Now in the invoice, we tick a free product tickbox so it shows the total price is 0(Zero).
