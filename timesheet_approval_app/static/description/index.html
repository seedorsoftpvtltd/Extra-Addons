Odoo Employee Timesheet Sheet and Timesheet Approval App

Employee Timesheet Approval Odoo apps helps to Employee to logged their timesheet and make their timesheet log sheet. Also employee able to submit their weekly/monthly timesheet for approval to their manager. Timesheet manager can check and approve timesheet easily with single approval and mass approval option .This Odoo module helps employee to manage Timesheet and manager will have approve or reject timesheet option.

Employee Access Rights

Employee Can Manager their Timesheets.

Approve and Reject Timesheet

Manager can Approve or Reject Timesheet of Employees.

Get Notification Mail of Approved and Rejected Timesheet.

Submit To Manager

Approve and Reject Timesheet

If the employee submits it than after it is not edited by the employee, the manager can approve or reject.
