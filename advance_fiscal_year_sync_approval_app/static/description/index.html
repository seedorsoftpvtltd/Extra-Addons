Odoo Advance Fiscal Year for Accounting with Approval App

After Odoo version8 odoo stop supporting Financial fiscal year and fiscal period setup and management for accounting system. With this odoo apps we support same feature so user can create their own fiscal year and peroid for accounting management and generate monthly or three monthly period for fiscal year and it also have feature to close fiscal financial year , transfer opening/closing entry etc. This is the advance Fiscal Year odoo apps which helps to manage double approval process for fiscal year and period, so whenever accountant want to open or close fiscal year and period it require double approval process which assigned selected user groups.
