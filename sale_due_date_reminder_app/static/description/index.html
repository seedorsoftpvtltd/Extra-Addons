Sale Due Date Reminder in Odoo

This apps helps to remind sale due date via email to all sales followers.

Send Reminder to all Sales Follower

All sales followers will notify by email on sale order due.

Set Due Date on Sale Order

User can set due date on sales order according to that due date reminder will be sent.

Email Time Interval

Followers will notify on different interval like two days before and on due date of order.
