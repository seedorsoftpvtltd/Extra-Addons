Employee Experience

This module adding feature for calculating employee's Experience

Employee Experience

Company Experience of employee

Total Experience of employee

Relevant Experience of employee

Attachment to add if any

Configuration in Resume tab

Relevant Experience -> It will indicate that this experience is relevant to employee's current job position or not.
Company Experience -> Calculating Experience of current company based on date range mentioned.
Total Experience -> Summation of all experiences.
Compnay Name -> Can enter company name incase employee have any previous experience.
Attachment -> Fields to attach documents.













**This module is compatible for both the vesrion enterprise and community.
