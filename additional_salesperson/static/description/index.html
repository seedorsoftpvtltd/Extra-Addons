Additional Salesperson on Contact, Sale Order, and Opportunity

You can add additional salesperson on Contact, Sale Order, or Opportunity. For example, You can to assign salesperson assistant to responsible on the entity besides the salesperson itself.

1. Additional Sales Person On Contact

You can assign multiple user as additional salesperson on contact.

2. Additional Sales Person On Sale Order

You can assign multiple user as additional salesperson on sale order. The additional salesperson will automatically re-assigned when you change the customer.

3. Additional Salesperson On Opportunity

You can assign multiple user as additional salesperson on opportunity. When you create new contact from the opportunity, the additional salesperson on contact will be assigned by value of the opportunity where the contact is created from.
