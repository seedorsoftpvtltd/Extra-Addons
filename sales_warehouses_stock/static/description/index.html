Show Warehouses Stock Quantity in Quotations

Concept and Business Scenario

When you are creating a quotation in the Sales, especially in front of your customer, you should be able to know how much you have of the selected product in different warehouses without going through a long process by opening different pages. So, here we are looking for a solution that will allow you to know the quantities in the other warehouses on the same page of the quotation.

Why this module?

This module will add the functionality of seeing all the stock in all warehouses from the Quotation directly which will solve the above business case.

Functionality

  • Adds the warehouses to the popup window in Quotations.
  • Dynamic detect of any new warehouses. (The module can work with any number of warehouses)
