Carry forward leaves

This module is used to lapse the leaves which are more than what can be carry forwarded

Automated job for leave carry forward at the year end.

Configuration for carry forward leaves.

Remaining leaves will be lapsed.

Configuration > Time off Types > Open any Leave type

Flag true field carry forward and define number of days to carry forward from particular leave type, other remaining allocated leaves will be lapsed.







Total Allocated leaves for employee.

Automated Job.

At the end of the year this automated job is called and lapse the leaves which are more than what can be carry forwarded.

If the leave balance is less than carry forward nothing will happen. But if the leave balance is more than carry forward, then extra leaves will be lapsed.




Lapsed leaves.

Lapse leave is leave requests only but it will have the checkbox lapse leaves checked. This will be auto confirmed, approved and validated by the scheduler itself.


Leave Balance for employee.

Check leave balance for employee after carry forward and leave lapsed.



**This module is compatible for both the vesrion enterprise and community.
