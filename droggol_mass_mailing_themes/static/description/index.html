Send emails with Your Brand Color

You will be able to configure the colors of the brand from the mass mailing configuration. You just need to configure your brand colors once and that all. All of the available snippets, buttons, background colors, and text colors will use your brand colors.

Send Mobile Responsive emails.

All the emails generated via our theme are mobile compatible. All of our snippets are carefully crafted for mobile. That's why our emails look stunning on mobile. The width of our email is adjusted automatically and multiple columns break into rows in mobile. This gives pleasing user-experience to the end-user.

Elegantly Crafted Options.

All of the snippet options are beautifully crafted. It automatically tasks the smart decision to save your time. Example for, when you change the background color of the snippet, buttons and text colors automatically updated to save your time. These options are also updated when you change the theme's color configuration.

Fill data Dynamically.

This theme comes with dynamic snippets. Example for, just select few products and product block will be generated automatically. These dynamic snippets are available for Products, Blogs and Events with multiple design options.

Add new Background Colors on the fly.

Once you have configured brand colors from settings, you can use your brand colors as a background and text color. But if you want new background colors, you can new background colors on the fly. These background colors can be used in all of the snippets including headers and footers.

Dynamic Separator & Border Options.

All of our new snippets are having the possibility of adding borders and separators. And it comes with several configurable options like border style, border position, colors, the thickness of the border.
