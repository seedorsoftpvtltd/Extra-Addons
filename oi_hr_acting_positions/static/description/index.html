HR Multiple Positions

"One Employee Holding Multiple Roles"

This module allows to assign multiple roles/job positions to one employee.

Description

Human resources departments often have a variety of job types and titles. Sometimes, specially in small or medium size organizations, one employee handles several job positions. Implementing such a scenario in Odoo can be confusing and complicated for HR managers. This module helps you to easily assign multiple job positions to one employee and then the corresponding department will be added automatically.
